About Gold Team

Gold Team is a team of eleven computer science students at UC San Diego. The team first formed in Spring 2015 in the course CSE 110 Software Engineering, taught by Massimiliano Menarini. When we were first developing ideas, we ran through many options such as an interactive pet game, a digital whiteboard application, and even an application to aid musicians. Ultimately, we created Triton Battles because we wanted to help students like us study material in a more effective, challenging, and entertaining way than the existing flashcard applications. The team that made Triton Battles possible consists of:
  • Annie Chen - Project Manager
  • Dylan Pererira - Technical Lead
  • Galen Krulce - Technical Lead
  • Boming Eric Cheng - Architect
  • Rodrigo Manzano - Architect
  • Alex Luu - Business Analyst
  • Jessica Yang - Business Analyst

  • Richard Zheng - Tester/Quality Assurance
  • Emily Shung - UI Designer
  • Hilary Louie - UI Designer
For 10 weeks, we put in hours of hard work and dedication in order to make Triton Battles what it is today. We hope you enjoy our product!
